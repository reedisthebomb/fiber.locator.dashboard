# Fiber Locator Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-01

Fiber Locator is a private field-work application for authorized locating crews. The app connects to the Fiber Locator dashboard to help users review assigned One Call tickets, view ticket maps, submit completion information, and upload supporting field attachments.

## Information The App Handles

Fiber Locator may handle:

- Account login information, such as username and password.
- Session cookies used to keep authorized users signed in.
- Ticket workflow information, including ticket numbers, ticket details, ticket status, locate actions, notes, and timestamps.
- User-selected photos or videos uploaded as ticket attachments.
- Device location permission for the map's optional `Locate me` feature.

The current `Locate me` feature is used to show the user's position on the in-app map. It is not uploaded to the Fiber Locator dashboard by the current Android app build.

## How Information Is Used

Information is used to provide the app's field workflow, including authentication, ticket review, map display, ticket completion, attachment upload, and dashboard synchronization.

## Sharing

Fiber Locator does not sell user data. Data is used for the private Fiber Locator dashboard workflow and is not intended for public sharing.

The app may load map tiles or related map resources from configured map providers to display map backgrounds and field context.

## Data Retention

Ticket workflow data and attachments are retained according to the Fiber Locator dashboard's operational needs. Authorized administrators may remove or correct records when required.

## Security

Access to the app requires an authorized Fiber Locator account. The production dashboard connection uses HTTPS for login, ticket sync, notes, and attachments.

## Contact

For privacy or account questions, contact the Fiber Locator administrator at the support email provided in the Google Play listing.
