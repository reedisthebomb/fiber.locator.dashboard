Fiber Locator Google Play Upload
================================

Yes, you need to update the native app in Google Play.

New app version:
versionCode 82
versionName 0.1.81

Run this on the DMI tablet in PowerShell:

scp linux@192.168.50.220:/home/linux/fiber.locator.dashboard/android-auto/app/build/outputs/bundle/release/app-release.aab "$env:USERPROFILE\Downloads\fiber-locator-0.1.81.aab"

Then upload this file to Google Play Console:

Downloads\fiber-locator-0.1.81.aab

SHA256:
707ce672c6369cc3177b1f88567cdbe24246dcd772c745b8a2ff41da90f6dc62

Note:
Android Auto live map is back to a clean 2D heading-up renderer. The fake tilt/perspective stretch is removed, stale headings fall back to north-up when stopped, duplicate map status text is removed, and Mapbox basemap tiles use sharper 512px style tiles while layers/tickets stay accurately aligned.
