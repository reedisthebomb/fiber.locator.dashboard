Fiber Locator Google Play Upload
================================

Yes, you need to update the native app in Google Play.

New app version:
versionCode 54
versionName 0.1.53

Run this on the DMI tablet in PowerShell:

scp linux@192.168.50.220:/home/linux/fiber.locator.dashboard/android-auto/app/build/outputs/bundle/release/app-release.aab "$env:USERPROFILE\Downloads\fiber-locator-0.1.53.aab"

Then upload this file to Google Play Console:

Downloads\fiber-locator-0.1.53.aab

SHA256:
153ac9c1bcc08c38f39a5cea5dc70a586e002eb1c36d8644a9290c32df892d6b

Note:
Every native Android ticket detail now has an Open Live GeoCall button. Contact and company phone numbers are blue tap-to-dial links that open the phone dialer without requiring call permission. Previous ticket completion photo upload, submit progress, Android Auto polygon styling, live-map ticket focus, mobile measure position, and Location Photos scroll fixes remain included.
