Fiber Locator Google Play Upload
================================

Yes, you need to update the native app in Google Play.

New app version:
versionCode 55
versionName 0.1.54

Run this on the DMI tablet in PowerShell:

scp linux@192.168.50.220:/home/linux/fiber.locator.dashboard/android-auto/app/build/outputs/bundle/release/app-release.aab "$env:USERPROFILE\Downloads\fiber-locator-0.1.54.aab"

Then upload this file to Google Play Console:

Downloads\fiber-locator-0.1.54.aab

SHA256:
491e139f29cdfb2ef323dd0f8a5a0e2cd899f04429fed0d31b557e766c3e28ac

Note:
Android Auto live map now has automatic night mode. It follows the Android Auto host dark mode and also has a lightweight civil-dusk/dawn fallback based on current/map location. At night it switches to Mapbox Navigation Night tiles when a Mapbox token is available, otherwise Carto dark raster tiles, and clears stale day tiles during the switch. Previous Live GeoCall, tap-to-dial, ticket completion photo upload, submit progress, Android Auto polygon styling, live-map ticket focus, mobile measure position, and Location Photos scroll fixes remain included.
