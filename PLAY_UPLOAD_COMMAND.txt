Fiber Locator Google Play Upload
================================

Yes, you need to update the native app in Google Play.

New app version:
versionCode 83
versionName 0.1.82

Run this on the DMI tablet in PowerShell:

scp linux@192.168.50.220:/home/linux/fiber.locator.dashboard/android-auto/app/build/outputs/bundle/release/app-release.aab "$env:USERPROFILE\Downloads\fiber-locator-0.1.82.aab"

Then upload this file to Google Play Console:

Downloads\fiber-locator-0.1.82.aab

SHA256:
a1dfc587d4cdb8b40b8664ff150917483c30a64365b191f9219cc92909565917

Note:
Android Auto live map keeps the clean 2D heading-up renderer, adds a safe Day/Night overlay toggle, starts closer to max zoom, preserves manual zoom, and smooths follow movement. The native phone app now uses a foreground ticket-completion service for attachment uploads, preserves multi-photo selection before submit, and sorts tickets by priority and due date.
