Fiber Locator Google Play Upload
================================

Yes, you need to update the native app in Google Play.

New app version:
versionCode 53
versionName 0.1.52

Run this on the DMI tablet in PowerShell:

scp linux@192.168.50.220:/home/linux/fiber.locator.dashboard/android-auto/app/build/outputs/bundle/release/app-release.aab "$env:USERPROFILE\Downloads\fiber-locator-0.1.52.aab"

Then upload this file to Google Play Console:

Downloads\fiber-locator-0.1.52.aab

SHA256:
e9e397851c5facc34cdd13893fa4f7f8b63b9524c687ef938834f70c1b260e83

Note:
Ticket completion photo uploads no longer depend on OneDrive. Regular ticket attachments are saved locally on the cloud dashboard under the ticket number, and the Android app now waits longer for multi-photo upload responses and shows the server's real error message if one occurs. The previous submit progress bar, Android Auto polygon styling, live-map ticket focus, mobile measure position, and Location Photos scroll fixes remain included.
