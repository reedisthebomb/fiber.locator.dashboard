Fiber Locator Google Play Upload
================================

Yes, you need to update the native app in Google Play.

New app version:
versionCode 48
versionName 0.1.47

Run this on the DMI tablet in PowerShell:

scp linux@192.168.50.220:/home/linux/fiber.locator.dashboard/android-auto/app/build/outputs/bundle/release/app-release.aab "$env:USERPROFILE\Downloads\fiber-locator-0.1.47.aab"

Then upload this file to Google Play Console:

Downloads\fiber-locator-0.1.47.aab

SHA256:
24c691d0cbcae814f42d712e1b9ec9dd484fc65c83269d680aa3a0254ace4c26

Note:
The native Dig Tickets page now keeps the toolbar/header fixed to its own space and leaves the ticket table scrollable vertically and horizontally in portrait and landscape. Android Auto Tickets now includes TCW/DMI tickets in the regular Tickets tab with the same TCW/DMI badge/color treatment, and the separate TCW tab is removed. Android Auto Dashboard Map now fits the selected ticket polygon instead of opening in live-follow mode, while the normal Map tab still follows live phone location. The Android Auto map also keeps the near-invisible ticket polygon fill, layer 28/42 size boosts, dashboard-style colors, high-accuracy fused location filtering, and adds throttled/offscreen-capped drawing to reduce pan/zoom freezes.
