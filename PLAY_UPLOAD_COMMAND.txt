Fiber Locator Google Play Upload
================================

Yes, you need to update the native app in Google Play.

New app version:
versionCode 51
versionName 0.1.50

Run this on the DMI tablet in PowerShell:

scp linux@192.168.50.220:/home/linux/fiber.locator.dashboard/android-auto/app/build/outputs/bundle/release/app-release.aab "$env:USERPROFILE\Downloads\fiber-locator-0.1.50.aab"

Then upload this file to Google Play Console:

Downloads\fiber-locator-0.1.50.aab

SHA256:
d0496ed9b23beac4e482a25c1b875737bbc3a23c6309beae9be2e2c794ea062b

Note:
The native completion form now shows an in-form progress bar/status while submitting and disables the controls until the request finishes. Android Auto ticket polygons now have a darker/thicker outline with a slightly stronger fill. The native app's "See on dashboard map" action opens the dashboard map focused on the ticket and keeps it there until you leave manually. The mobile dashboard measure control is moved up away from the phone navigation area, and the Location Photos view is its own scrollable container. The web dashboard cache was bumped to 20260618131149 and deployed.
