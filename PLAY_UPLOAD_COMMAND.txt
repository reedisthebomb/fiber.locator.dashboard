Fiber Locator Google Play Upload
================================

Yes, you need to update the native app in Google Play.

New app version:
versionCode 52
versionName 0.1.51

Run this on the DMI tablet in PowerShell:

scp linux@192.168.50.220:/home/linux/fiber.locator.dashboard/android-auto/app/build/outputs/bundle/release/app-release.aab "$env:USERPROFILE\Downloads\fiber-locator-0.1.51.aab"

Then upload this file to Google Play Console:

Downloads\fiber-locator-0.1.51.aab

SHA256:
4acfad9844c4d4c50b3a98b7f0b83607af05a357c8135a0611f336f4006334b4

Note:
The native completion form still shows an in-form progress bar/status while submitting. Android Auto ticket polygons still have a darker/thicker outline with a slightly stronger fill. The native app's "See on dashboard map" action now opens the native live map focused on the job and keeps the app on that live map until you manually leave it. The live map also fits the selected polygon when the ticket has polygon data instead of a single coordinate. The mobile dashboard measure control is moved up away from the phone navigation area, and the Location Photos view is its own scrollable container.
